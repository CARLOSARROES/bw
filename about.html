About

Blakemore Walker was set up in 2003 by chartered surveyor Clive Robbins. The company provides independent property and business advice to clients across the northern Spanish regions. Blakemore Walker advises on all property purchase and sales matters as well as providing technical and legal advice for obtaining planning permissions, carrying out construction projects and the general management of land.

Many of our clients contact us once they have already found a property and under these circumstances we are able to deal with the agents or owners as necessary and advise on all the practical, tecnical and legal issues through to completion.
